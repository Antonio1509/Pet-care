About PetCare

Learn about our mission and team.

Hello there pet parents!

We love pets, they mean the world to us. That's why here at PetCare, we provide loving, professional pet care services tailored to each animal's needs. Our team is trained in safe handling, first aid, and compassionate care.

At PetCare, our main goal is to provide our customers with easy access to quality pet services, products and expert answers to all your pet care related questions. Our online store is an innovative online retail and service store that ensures convienient and easy access to budget friendly, and good quality services and products.

Our Values

Pet Grooming

Grooming is fundamental at PetCare. Our selected stores offer professional grooming for your pets, focused on their well-being and your satisfaction.

Can't visit our selected stores? Don't fret, we have a mobile pet grooming parlour! Grooming should be convienient and stress free for both pets and their parents. Our groomers use top of the range products and equipment to ensure that your pets leave looking and feeling fantastic.
